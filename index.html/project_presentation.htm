The Pirahna Pipe

Presentation project

Encodeur: Noah 3D, web developer: Marius

Needs analysis

The customer wanted a system that keeps the soil moist for the plants. A system that is self-sufficient in energy and water for at least 15 days The system must contain a maximum of five plants, while remaining discreet and blending in with the other flower pots.

The project plan

This is the plan of the project with the different angles of view. As part of the project, we have a water tank, a water pump, a water level sensor, a Microbit card or Arduino card, a gesture detector, an indicator, a humidity detector, and finally the cover for the water tank and flower pot.

Project conclusion

During the product creation process, we encountered several problems. These included issues related to coding, the motherboard, and 3D design, as well as a change in the board after the first cut. Problems related to thickness led to a change in the orientation of the top of the plant pot cover. We also experienced delays due to absences. A cover could be added to the product to cover the cables and water reservoir.
